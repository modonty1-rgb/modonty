## Objection Responses

**"It's expensive / Limited budget"**
→ You're not paying separate writers, designers, and SEO experts. Compare: one agency article costs more than our entire tier. Start with Basic tier and upgrade when you see results.

**"I don't have time to review content"**
→ Your role: approval only. We write → send for review → you approve → we publish. Minimal effort, full control.

**"Google doesn't show me at all"**
→ That's exactly what we fix. SEO-optimized articles + Schema data + proper page structure. Results improve as we publish consistently.

**"I'm not sure real customers will come"**
→ Dashboard shows Hot/Warm/Cold scoring. You see who spent time reading, who clicked "Ask Company", who subscribed to your newsletter. Real engagement data.

**"I already have a website"**
→ Our client profile complements your site. We drive visitors from articles to your site button. Modonty = content + reach; your site = the product/service.

**"Let me think / I'll get back to you"**
→ "Makes sense. Want to schedule a short call next week where I show you the dashboard and you decide?" or "What question is stopping you from trying it today?"
