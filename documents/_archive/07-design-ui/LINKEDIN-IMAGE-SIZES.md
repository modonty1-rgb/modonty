# LinkedIn Image Sizes – Complete Inventory

Complete list of image dimensions from official LinkedIn documentation. Use for client assets, ads, events, and content.

**Design tips:** Prefer high-resolution JPEG. Place important content in center; keep key details away from edges (avoid lower-right cropping). Test logos on light and dark backgrounds.

---

## 1. LinkedIn Pages & Career Pages

**File rules:** PNG or JPEG only. **Max file size: 3 MB.**

- **Page logo**
  - Min: 268 × 268 px
  - Recommended: 400 × 400 px
  - Square. Test on light and dark backgrounds.

- **Page cover image**
  - Recommended: 4200 × 700 px (6:1 ratio)
  - Center important content; may be cropped by device.

- **Life tab – main image**
  - Recommended: 1128 × 376 px (3:1 ratio)

- **Life tab – custom modules**
  - Recommended: 502 × 282 px

- **Life tab – company photos**
  - Min: 264 × 176 px
  - Recommended: 900 × 600 px

---

## 2. Page post with URL (link preview)

- **Ratio:** 1.91:1
- **Recommended:** 1200 × 627 px
- **Minimum width:** 200 px

---

## 3. Personal profile

**File rules:** JPG or PNG. **Max file size: 8 MB.**

- **Profile photo**
  - Size: 400 × 400 px to 7680 × 4320 px

- **Background (cover) photo**
  - Recommended: 1584 × 396 px (4:1 ratio)

---

## 4. Share photos (feed posts)

**File rules:** JPG or PNG. **Max size per upload: 5 MB.**

- **Single or multi-photo post**
  - Min: 552 × 276 px
  - Recommended width: 1080 px
  - Aspect ratio: 3:1 to 4:5

---

## 5. Single image ads (Sponsored Content)

**File rules:** JPG, PNG, or GIF. **Max file size: 5 MB.**

- **Horizontal / landscape (1.91:1)** (recommended)
  - Min: 640 × 360 px
  - Max: 7680 × 4320 px

- **Square (1:1)**
  - Min: 360 × 360 px
  - Max: 4320 × 4320 px

- **Vertical (4:5)** (recommended for mobile)
  - Min: 360 × 640 px
  - Max: 2430 × 4320 px

---

## 6. Events (event cover image)

- **Event cover / banner**
  - Use 480 × 270 px or 1280 × 720 px
  - Min width: 480 px
  - Aspect ratio: 16:9

---

## 7. Newsletter

- **Newsletter logo**
  - Recommended: 300 × 300 px

- **Cover photo per article**
  - Recommended: 1920 × 1080 px

---

## 8. Articles (publish on LinkedIn)

- **Article cover image**
  - Optimal: 1920 × 1080 px (16:9)
  - File types: JPEG, JPG, PNG, WEBP
  - GIF not supported for cover

---

- **Page logo:** 400 × 400 px
- **Page cover:** 4200 × 700 px (6:1)
- **Life tab main:** 1128 × 376 px (3:1)
- **Life tab custom modules:** 502 × 282 px
- **Life tab company photos:** 900 × 600 px
- **Page post (URL preview):** 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1)
- **Profile photo:** 400 × 400 px minimum
- **Profile background:** 1584 × 396 px (4:1)
- **Share photos (feed):** 1080 px width, aspect 3:1 to 4:5
- **Single image ad (landscape):** 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1)
- **Single image ad (vertical):** 1080 × 1350 px (4:5)
- **Event cover:** 1280 × 720 px (16:9)
- **Newsletter logo:** 300 × 300 px
- **Newsletter article cover:** 1920 × 1080 px (16:9)
- **Article cover:** 1920 × 1080 px (16:9)
