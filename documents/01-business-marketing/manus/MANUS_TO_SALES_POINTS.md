# Manus Analysis to Sales Points

Quick reference for converting analysis documents to sales messages.

---

## Key Insights from Manus Analysis

1. **Site Structure**: Multi-section layout with profile, articles, analytics
2. **Features**: Comments, followers, reviews, lead scoring, competitor tracking
3. **User Journey**: Sign up → provide business info → articles deliver → see results → renew

---

## Primary Sales Messages

**Message 1**: "All your customer communication in one place—messages, newsletter, reviews—no more scattered emails"

**Message 2**: "Know exactly who's ready to buy—lead scoring shows Hot (ready), Warm (nurture), Cold (learning)"

**Message 3**: "We write and design everything—you never provide designs or content"

**Message 4**: "See real results in your dashboard—page views, engagement, conversions, not vague metrics"

**Message 5**: "18 months for 12 months payment—we believe in giving you time to see SEO impact"

---

## Tier Messaging

- **Basic**: Start with 2 articles/month + profile
- **Standard**: 4 articles + advanced analytics + followers + reviews
- **Pro**: 8 articles + lead scoring + competitor tracking + verified badge
- **Premium**: 12 articles + everything + account manager + CMS integration

**E-commerce**: "See which product articles bring actual customers (lead scoring)"
