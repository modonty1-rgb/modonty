# Modonty Launch Strategy & Pricing

## Value Proposition

Modonty is a **high-performance, SEO-first Growth Engine** designed for modern businesses. Unlike traditional CMS platforms that focus on content management, Modonty focuses on **content performance** and automatically tracks real user engagement.

---

## Why Customers Will Pay

### 1. Intelligent Lead Scoring (The "Money" Feature)

Most analytics show how many people visited. Modonty shows **who is ready to buy**.

**The Algorithm**: Automatically calculates 0-100 score based on:
- 25% Frequency: How often they return
- 25% Depth: Active reading time (not just tab open)
- 25% Interaction: Scroll, click, share behavior
- 25% Conversion: Key event triggering

**The Outcome**: Filter leads by HOT (>70), WARM (>40), or COLD to focus sales efforts.

### 2. Technical SEO on Autopilot

- **Auto-JSON-LD**: Generates Google-compliant structured data (Articles, Organizations, FAQs, Breadcrumbs)
- **Dynamic Meta Tags**: Auto-generates og:image, twitter:card, canonical URLs
- **No manual SEO work needed**

### 3. Core Web Vitals Monitoring

Monitors LCP, CLS, INP in real-time for every page view. Clients see exactly how their site performs.

### 4. Social Proof Ecosystem

- **Verified Reviews**: Aggregates comments into central "Reviews" tab
- **Follower System**: Users can follow brands, building re-targetable audience
- **Engagement Loops**: "Facepile" displays create FOMO and trust

## Pricing Model (4-Tier SaaS)

### STARTER
- **Price**: Free or $9/month
- **Articles**: 2/month
- **Users**: 1 seat
- **Features**: Basic CMS editor, page views analytics, standard SEO meta tags, public profile

### GROWTH
- **Price**: $29-49/month
- **Articles**: 4-8/month
- **Users**: 3 seats
- **Features**: Advanced analytics (scroll depth, time on page), social features (reviews, likes, followers), custom branding, priority support

### PROFESSIONAL (Sweet Spot)
- **Price**: $79-99/month
- **Articles**: Unlimited
- **Users**: 10 seats
- **Features**: Verified blue checkmark, lead scoring (Hot/Warm/Cold), Core Web Vitals, AI content assistant, conversion tracking

### ENTERPRISE
- **Price**: Custom
- **Features**: Unlimited everything, dedicated account manager, custom integrations, SLA/uptime guarantees, SSO

- Real-time dashboard, lead scoring, scroll depth, device/geo breakdown, UTM tracking

**Content & Media**

**Social & Community**

**Technical & Integrations**
