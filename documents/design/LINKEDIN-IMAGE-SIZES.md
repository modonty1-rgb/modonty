# LinkedIn Image Sizes – Full Inventory (Designer Guideline)

Complete list of image dimensions and file rules from **official LinkedIn Help and Marketing Solutions** documentation. Use this for client assets, ads, events, and content so exports match LinkedIn’s requirements.

**Design tips (apply where relevant):** Prefer high-resolution JPEG for best display. Place important content in the **center**; keep key details **away from edges** (especially lower-right), as images may be cropped on different devices. Logos are shown on light and dark backgrounds—test both.

---

## 1. LinkedIn Pages & Career Pages (company / client pages)

**File rules:** PNG or JPEG only. **Max file size: 3 MB.**  
**Source:** [Image specifications for your LinkedIn Pages and Career Pages](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a563309)

- **Page logo**
  - Minimum: 268 × 268 px
  - Recommended: 400 × 400 px
  - Square. Test on light and dark (transparency shows on white).
- **Page cover image**
  - Recommended: 4200 × 700 px
  - Ratio 6:1. May be cropped by device; center important content.
- **Life tab – main image**
  - Recommended: 1128 × 376 px
  - Ratio 3:1.
- **Life tab – custom modules**
  - Recommended: 502 × 282 px
- **Life tab – company photos**
  - Minimum: 264 × 176 px
  - Recommended: 900 × 600 px
  - Larger = better when member clicks to enlarge.

**Dynamic cover (Premium Company Page):** Same as Page cover; single image or up to 5 images for slideshow. [Add dynamic cover images](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a7433061)

---

## 2. Page post with URL (link preview in feed)

When adding a **custom image to a Page post with a URL** (link preview):

- **Ratio:** 1.91:1
- **Recommended:** 1200 × 627 px
- **Minimum width:** 200 px (images under 200 px wide show as a thumbnail on the left).
- **Note:** Does not apply to “image only” or “image + text” posts; those use the share-photo specs below.

**Source:** [Image specifications for your LinkedIn Pages and Career Pages](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a563309) (Custom image specifications section)

---

## 3. Personal profile

**File rules:** JPG or PNG. **Max file size: 8 MB.**  
**Source:** [Photo won't upload to your profile](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a549049), [Add or change the cover image on your profile](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a568217)

- **Profile photo**
  - Size: 400 × 400 px to 7680 × 4320 px
  - Use a photo that won’t need much cropping.
- **Background (cover) photo**
  - Recommended: 1584 × 396 px
  - Ratio 4:1. Appears behind profile photo in intro section.

---

## 4. Share photos (feed posts – uploaded photos)

**File rules:** JPG or PNG. **Max size per upload: 5 MB.**  
**Source:** [Share photos on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a527229)

- **Single or multi-photo post**
  - Minimum: 552 × 276 px
  - Recommended width: 1080 px
  - Aspect ratio: 3:1 to 4:5 (width:height). If larger, image is centered and cropped to max ratio.
- **Multi-photo posts**
  - Max ratio 4:5 (e.g. 640 × 800 px). First image drives layout. Max 20 photos. Layout and cropping depend on first image orientation.

---

## 5. Single image ads (Sponsored Content)

**File rules:** JPG, PNG, or GIF (animated GIF max 250 frames). **Max file size: 5 MB.** Max dimensions 7680 × 4320 px. Images under 401 px wide display as thumbnails.  
**Source:** [Single image ads advertising specifications](https://www.linkedin.com/help/lms/answer/a426534)

- **Horizontal / landscape 1.91:1** (recommended)
  - Minimum: 640 × 360 px
  - Maximum: 7680 × 4320 px
- **Square 1:1**
  - Minimum: 360 × 360 px
  - Maximum: 4320 × 4320 px
  - May be cropped when shared organically.
- **Vertical 4:5** (recommended for mobile)
  - Minimum: 360 × 640 px
  - Maximum: 2430 × 4320 px
  - Vertical 1:1.91 gets borders; 4:5 avoids them.

**Tip:** For thumbnails, aspect between 3:2 and 16:9 minimizes cropping.

---

## 6. Events (event cover image)

**Source:** [Create a LinkedIn Event](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a554183), [Make the most of your LinkedIn Events](https://www.linkedin.com/help/lms/answer/a564536)

- **Event cover / banner**
  - Use 480 × 270 px or 1280 × 720 px
  - Min width: 480 px. Aspect ratio 16:9. Upload from device only (no cloud).

---

## 7. Newsletter

**Source:** [LinkedIn Newsletters best practices](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a517940)

- **Newsletter logo**
  - Recommended: 300 × 300 px
- **Cover photo per article**
  - Recommended: 1920 × 1080 px (per newsletter edition/article)

---

## 8. Articles (publish on LinkedIn)

**Source:** [Add and edit images or videos, cover images, and other rich media to your article](https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a521719)

- **Article cover image**
  - Optimal: 1920 × 1080 px
  - File types: JPEG, JPG, PNG, WEBP. Cropped to 16:9. GIF not supported for cover.
- **In-article images:** GIF, JPEG, JPG, PNG, WEBP supported.

---

## Quick reference (recommended sizes only)

- **Page logo:** 400 × 400 px (1:1), max 3 MB
- **Page cover:** 4200 × 700 px (6:1), max 3 MB
- **Life tab main:** 1128 × 376 px (3:1), max 3 MB
- **Life tab custom modules:** 502 × 282 px, max 3 MB
- **Life tab company photos:** 900 × 600 px, max 3 MB
- **Page post (URL preview):** 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1)
- **Profile photo:** 400 × 400 px minimum, max 8 MB
- **Profile background:** 1584 × 396 px (4:1), max 8 MB
- **Share photos (feed):** 1080 px width minimum, aspect 3:1 to 4:5, max 5 MB
- **Single image ad (landscape):** e.g. 1200 × 627 px (1.91:1), max 5 MB
- **Single image ad (vertical):** e.g. 1080 × 1350 px (4:5), max 5 MB
- **Event cover:** 1280 × 720 px (16:9)
- **Newsletter logo:** 300 × 300 px
- **Newsletter article cover:** 1920 × 1080 px (16:9)
- **Article cover:** 1920 × 1080 px (16:9)

## Modonty alignment

The app content width **max-w-[1128px]** matches LinkedIn’s Life tab main image width (1128 px) so client assets that follow the Life tab specs display consistently.
