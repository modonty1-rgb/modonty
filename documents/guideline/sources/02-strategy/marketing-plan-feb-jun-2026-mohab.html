Build the first human-only SEO and content platform designed for MENA businesses

A Saudi-first platform focused on Egypt and KSA in 2026. We help businesses that cannot rank on Google (or need high-quality backlinks and real traffic) by publishing human-written, intent-driven content with analysis and tracking. We also activate users through a coin and voucher system based on real reading behavior.

Winning idea

What we refuse to do

AI-generated writing, spam backlinks, fake traffic, low-quality content farms.

  • Trust: reputation-safe content for brands and professionals.
  • Proof: measurable reading-time and engagement signals.

What we do differently

We satisfy real search intent with deep analysis, and we track real user behavior to prove value.

  • Intent: content aligned to user queries and decision stages.
  • Tracking: session time, scroll depth, CTR, retention.

How we scale without losing quality

A strict editorial system, reusable templates, and a clear workflow between writing, design, video and distribution.

  • Playbooks: briefs, checklists, and reviews.
  • Repurpose: one article becomes many short assets.

Three offers, one ecosystem

Professionals create authority content. Businesses fund campaigns and vouchers. Readers create measurable engagement.

Professionals

We publish a human-written Google-optimized article about each professional. Free until 31 March. Paid onboarding starts 1 April.

  • Input: structured interview + proof + portfolio links.
  • Output: profile article + indexable page + internal linking.
  • Future upsells: featured slots, monthly updates, reputation monitoring.

Businesses

We deliver high-quality backlinks, real traffic, and measurable user engagement, plus voucher budget conversion to capture interested customers.

  • Value: lower cost than ads for qualified retargeting audiences.
  • Data: readership insights, clicks, and intent signals.
  • Sales: meetings booked by marketing, closed by BD.

Readers

Coin system that rewards real reading. 1 coin per blog read (2–3 minutes). Redeem coins as items/vouchers from partners.

  • Rule: track reading time, scroll depth, and behavior to prevent abuse.
  • Growth: challenges, referrals, community loops.
  • Outcome: retention and measurable value for businesses.

Weekly rhythm

A predictable cadence reduces startup chaos. Everyone knows what happens when.

Sunday: Planning and alignment (60–90 minutes)

  • Marketing Manager: weekly priorities, KPIs, and experiments.
  • Account Manager: editorial calendar and backlog status.
  • Designer and Video: production batch plan.
  • BD and Product: pipeline status, objections, product blockers.

Daily: Execution and handoffs

  • Content: write, review, publish, repurpose.
  • Distribution: post and engage on each platform.
  • Leads: route within 24 hours, BD contacts same day.
  • Tracking: monitor spend, CPL, meeting bookings.

Thursday: Performance review (45–60 minutes)

  • Review: followers, engagement, leads, meetings booked.
  • Decide: what to repeat, what to cut, what to test next.
  • BD feedback: objections and what collateral is needed.

Friday: Documentation (30 minutes)

  • Update: task board, done list, blockers.
  • Archive: top content, top hooks, top creatives.
  • Prepare: next week brief for faster start.

Key risks

The biggest risk is a reward system that attracts abuse. The second risk is content quality drop while scaling.

Risk 1: Coins abused by low-intent users

  • Safeguard: enforce time threshold, scroll depth, and behavior checks.
  • Safeguard: limit daily coins, use cooldowns, require account verification.
  • Safeguard: redemption requires verified contact and partner proof.

Risk 2: Content quality and trust degrade

  • Safeguard: strict human editorial checklist and approvals.
  • Safeguard: templates for structure, not for writing shortcuts.
  • Safeguard: publish fewer but better, with strong internal links.
