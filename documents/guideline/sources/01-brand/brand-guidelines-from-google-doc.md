## Brand Positioning

### Purpose
Enabling entrepreneurs in Saudi Arabia to grow through Google without stress and without complexity, through reliable content that reduces their learning time and agency cost.

### Vision
To become the most trusted Arabic reference for SaaS decisions, growth and visibility in Google within the Saudi context. Where people come to understand first... then the results come as a natural result.

### Mission
Producing specialized Arabic content that takes into account the Saudi market, targeting high-intentioned search words. It includes thoughtful links to subscriber sites, to build presence, trust, and long-term cumulative ranking.

## Logo Rules

### Three logo forms
1. **Logomark** — the iconic symbol (for compact spaces: social media avatars, favicons)
2. **Logotype** — the brand name in custom typographic form (primary identifier)
3. **Full Logo** — wordmark + logomark combined lockup (default, most used)

### Minimum sizes
| Form | Print | Digital |
|---|---|---|
| Logomark | 10mm | 50px |
| Logotype | 50mm | 150px |
| Full Logo | 50mm | 150px |

### Clearspace
A protected breathing-room area must surround the logo, kept free of any other visual elements.

### Logo Misuse — DO NOT
- ❌ Stretch the logo
- ❌ Rotate the logo
- ❌ Add containers
- ❌ Flip horizontally
- ❌ Flip vertically
- ❌ Shear the logo
- ❌ Re-color or alter

### Logo on backgrounds
Always select a background color or image that provides sufficient contrast.

## Visual Elements

### Brand Pattern
A unique graphic element used as background or accent texture to reinforce brand identity in a subtle, engaging way.

### Icons
- Designed for clear, simple communication
- Consistent visual style across all platforms
- Used to enhance usability and guide users

## Photography Direction

- **Authentic, vibrant, human-centric** — capture genuine moments
- Bright, natural lighting
- Avoid: overly staged, poorly lit, generic, or heavily filtered photography
- All imagery should feel authentic and align with brand personality
- Social media size: 1048×1048

## Corporate Assets

- **Letterhead & Document Covers:** use official templates only
- **Business Card:** clean layout + core brand elements
