# SEO Client Intake Questionnaire

**Purpose:** Senior SEO specialist discovery interview to gather complete information for comprehensive optimization and top-10 rankings.

---

## 1. Business & Industry

1. What is your core business? Describe in one sentence.
2. What industry/sector do you operate in?
3. Who are your top 3 direct competitors?
4. What makes your business unique? (Unique selling proposition)
5. What is your business model? (B2B, B2C, both, SaaS, e-commerce, etc.)
6. What is your target market? (Geography, language, demographic)
7. What is your annual revenue range? (Helps prioritize investment level)
8. How long has the business been operating?

---

## 2. Goals & KPIs

9. What are your primary SEO goals? (Traffic, leads, sales, brand awareness)
10. What does "success" look like in 6 months? 12 months?
11. Do you have existing traffic/conversion benchmarks we should beat?
12. What is your current website traffic? (Monthly unique visitors)
13. What is your average conversion rate? (Lead, sign-up, purchase)
14. What is the lifetime value of a typical customer?
15. Are you tracking conversions now? What tools? (GA4, Hotjar, etc.)

---

## 3. Target Audience

16. Who is your ideal customer? (Persona: age, job, pain points)
17. What problems does your product/service solve for them?
18. What keywords do you think customers use when searching for you?
19. At what stage of the buyer journey are you targeting? (Awareness, consideration, decision)
20. What language(s) does your audience use?
21. Is your audience mobile-first or desktop-first?
22. What content formats do they prefer? (Blog, video, guides, case studies)

---

## 4. Technical & Current State

23. What is your current domain? How long has it been active?
24. Do you have a staging/test environment?
25. What CMS/platform is your site built on? (WordPress, Next.js, custom, etc.)
26. Have you had any penalties from Google? (Manual actions, algorithm drops)
27. Do you have access to Google Search Console? Any current issues?
28. Is your site HTTPS? Mobile-friendly?
29. What is your current page speed? (Core Web Vitals, LCP, CLS, INP)
30. Do you have XML sitemaps? Robots.txt configured?
31. Are there any crawl/technical barriers? (JavaScript-heavy, blocked resources)
32. Do you have multiple domains/subdomains? How are they related?

---

## 5. Keywords & Content

33. What are your top 5–10 target keywords right now?
34. What keywords do you currently rank for? (Share GSC or Ahrefs data)
35. What keywords do competitors rank for that you don't?
36. Are there seasonal trends in your industry?
37. What content do you already have? (Blog posts, guides, product pages)
38. How often do you publish new content? Who creates it?
39. Do you have a content calendar or editorial process?
40. What topics does your audience care about most?
41. Are you targeting informational, transactional, or navigational keywords?
42. Do you want to target long-tail or head terms (or both)?

---

## 6. On-Page & Structure

43. What is your current site structure? (Main categories, subpages)
44. How many pages does your site have?
45. Are product/service pages optimized with unique titles and meta descriptions?
46. Do you use schema markup? (Organization, Product, Article, FAQ, etc.)
47. How are your URLs structured? (Clean, readable, keyword-rich?)
48. Do you have duplicate or thin content issues?
49. What is your internal linking strategy?
50. Do you have breadcrumbs, logical hierarchy, clear CTAs?

---

## 7. Local SEO (if applicable)

51. Do you have physical locations? How many?
52. Are you listed on Google Business Profile? Optimized?
53. Do you serve specific cities/regions?
54. What is your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across the web?
55. Do you have local citations? (Directories, industry listings)
56. Do you collect and display customer reviews?
57. Do you need local landing pages for different areas?

---

## 8. E-Commerce (if applicable)

58. How many products do you have?
59. Do product pages have unique titles, descriptions, images?
60. How is your checkout flow? (Cart abandonment rate?)
61. Do you use faceted navigation? How is it handled for SEO?
62. Do you have category pages? How are they optimized?
63. Are you using structured data for products? (Product schema)
64. Do you have reviews/ratings on product pages?

---

## 9. Link Building & Authority

65. What is your current domain authority/DR? (Ahrefs, Moz, etc.)
66. How many backlinks do you have? Quality vs. quantity?
67. Have you done any link building in the past? What strategies?
68. Are there any toxic or spammy links we need to disavow?
69. What partnerships or collaborations could produce natural links?
70. Do you have PR, partnerships, or industry associations?
71. Can you create linkable assets? (Research, tools, guides, data)

---

## 10. Brand & Social

72. What is your brand voice and tone?
73. Are you active on social media? Which platforms?
74. Do social profiles drive traffic or conversions?
75. How do social and SEO align in your strategy?
76. Do you have a strong brand name? Recognizable in your niche?

---

## 11. Competitors & Market

77. Who ranks #1 for your main keywords? Why do you think they win?
78. What content do top competitors have that you don't?
79. What is their backlink profile like?
80. Are there content gaps we can exploit?
81. What is the search volume for your main keywords?
82. How competitive is your market? (Low, medium, high)
83. Are there new or emerging competitors?

---

## 12. Resources & Constraints

84. What is your SEO budget? (Monthly/annually)
85. Do you have in-house developers? Designers? Writers?
86. What tools do you currently use? (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Screaming Frog, etc.)
87. Who will own SEO internally? (Point of contact)
88. How quickly can you implement technical changes?
89. Are there compliance or legal constraints? (Healthcare, finance, etc.)
90. Do you have a style guide or brand guidelines for content?

---

## 13. Historical & Context

91. Have you done SEO before? What worked? What didn't?
92. Have you migrated the site or changed domains? When?
93. Any major site redesigns or platform changes?
94. What was your peak traffic period? What changed?
95. Do you have access to historical analytics data?

---

## 14. International (if applicable)

96. Do you target multiple countries/languages?
97. Do you use hreflang? Correct implementation?
98. Are you using ccTLDs, subdirectories, or subdomains?
99. Which regions drive the most revenue?
100. Are there geo-specific content or product differences?

---

## Summary Checklist for Top-10 Strategy

- [ ] Clear business goals and KPIs defined
- [ ] Target audience and personas documented
- [ ] Keyword research completed (head + long-tail)
- [ ] Technical audit completed and issues fixed
- [ ] Content gap analysis vs. competitors
- [ ] On-page optimization plan for priority pages
- [ ] Link building and authority strategy
- [ ] Local SEO optimized (if applicable)
- [ ] Schema and structured data implemented
- [ ] Monitoring and reporting framework in place
